This recipe is my favorite.

  • Make the Chili Oil
  • Prep the Meat

Ingredients Required

Chili Oil

  • 2 tablespoons Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 inch-long piece of cinnamon
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup crushed red pepper flakes

For the meat

  • 3 teaspoons oil
  • 8 oz. veggie crumbles
  • 2 teaspoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 teaspoons shaoxing wine
  • 1 teaspoon dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon five spice powder

For the sauce

  • 2 tablespoons sesame paste
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons suger
  • 1/4 teaspoon five spice powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1/2 of the prepared chili oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup hot cooking water from the boiling noodles

For the noodles and vegetables

  • 1 lb fresh or dried white noodles
  • 1 small bunch leafy greens (baby bok choi)
  • chopped peanuts
  • chopped scallion
